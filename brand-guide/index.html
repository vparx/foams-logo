Graphic Elements

Our graphic language is built around two distinct visual styles. Each brings a different energy — use them individually or in combination.

Pink Vectors

Bold magenta lines, curves, and geometric forms. Use on dark backgrounds for maximum impact.

Focus Light

Concentrated light effects, glows, and radial highlights creating depth and drama.

Photos & Images

Our imagery is organized into three visual territories, each capturing a different dimension of the FOAMS world.

Tropical

Images of plants, forest, lush natural environments.

Use with: Focus Light only

Water

Foaming waves, water in motion, aquatic textures.

Use with: Pink Vectors only

Body

People, performers, human presence and movement.

Use with: Pink Vectors OR Focus Light — never both
