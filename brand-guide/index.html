Graphic Elements

Our graphic language is built around two distinct visual styles. Each brings a different energy — use them individually or in combination.

Pink Vectors

Bold magenta lines, curves, and geometric forms. Use on dark backgrounds for maximum impact.

✅ Pair with: Water images only

Focus Light

Concentrated light effects, glows, and radial highlights creating depth and drama.

✅ Pair with: Tropical images only

How to Combine Elements + Images

Each graphic element style is designed to pair with a specific image category. Follow these rules for a consistent and intentional visual result.

✅ Pink Vectors pair exclusively with Water images
Pink Vectors → Water
The electric energy of Pink Vectors matches the raw movement of water. Never use Pink Vectors with Tropical or Body images.
✅ Focus Light pairs exclusively with Tropical images
Focus Light → Tropical
The soft glows of Focus Light complement the organic textures of tropical nature. Never use Focus Light with Water or Body images.
⚠️ Both can be used with Body images — but never at the same time
Pink Vectors or Focus Light → Body
Choose one or the other — never mix Pink Vectors and Focus Light in the same composition.
💡 Recommended: use only ONE graphic element per image
More than one element is possible, but use it sparingly and with intention. Too many elements in one composition quickly becomes overwhelming.
🎨 Blending Modes are welcome — use with discretion
Feel free to use blending modes like Multiply, Overlay, Screen, etc. when it works well with the composition. However, use it with discretion — don't make it too strong or it will overpower the image.

Photos & Images

Our imagery is organized into three visual territories, each capturing a different dimension of the FOAMS world.

Tropical

Images of plants, forest, lush natural environments.

✅ Use with: Focus Light only

Water

Foaming waves, water in motion, aquatic textures.

✅ Use with: Pink Vectors only

Body

People, performers, human presence and movement.

⚠️ Use with: Pink Vectors OR Focus Light — never both

PROMPT GUIDELINES

Use estas diretrizes ao usar uma ferramenta generativa com prompt:
  • O logo deve ser posicionado em um local agradável, como em tríade. Não exatamente no centro
  • Use a imagem e o elemento gráfico apenas uma vez cada
  • O elemento gráfico deve estar sobre a imagem, não separado
  • Sinta-se livre para usar modos de mesclagem, mas não é obrigatório
  • Mantenha a vibe elegante, moderna e um pouco misteriosa
  • Para paisagem use resolução: 1920 × 1080
  • Para retrato use resolução: 1080 × 1920

Keep it fluid.

FOAMS is a living brand. The logo shifts, the images breathe, and the elements flow. These guidelines exist not to constrain but to give you a foundation to explore from.

When in doubt: stay bold, stay fluid, and use the generator.
